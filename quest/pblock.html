The p-block elements, encompassing groups 13 to 18, exhibit diverse properties due to their valence electrons occupying p-orbitals. This section introduces the p-block, highlighting trends in ionization energy, electronegativity, and atomic radii. We'll explore the unique characteristics of each group, from the metalloids in group 13 to the noble gases in group 18, laying the foundation for understanding their chemical behavior.
